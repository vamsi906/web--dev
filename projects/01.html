The King Legacy

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is one of the most celebrated cricketers in the world. Known for his aggressive batting and unwavering determination, Kohli made his international debut in 2008 and quickly established himself as a cornerstone of Indian cricket. A prolific right-handed batsman, he has set numerous records, including being the fastest to score 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 runs in ODIs. Kohli also captained India across all formats, becoming the nation’s most successful Test captain with 40 wins. Beyond his on-field exploits, he is a fitness icon, entrepreneur, and philanthropist, earning admiration globally for his passion and leadership.

Career Highlights

  • International debut
    • ODI: August 18, 2008, vs. Sri Lanka
    • Test: June 20, 2011, vs. West Indies
    • T20I: June 12, 2010, vs. Zimbabwe
  • Captaincy
    • Captained India in all three formats (2013–2022).
    • Most successful Test captain for India with 40 wins out of 68 matches.
